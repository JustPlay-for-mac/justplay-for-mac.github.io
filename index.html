JustPlay for Mac

JustPlay is a simple media player that plays any video or audio file on your Mac. It supports MP4, AVI, MOV, SWF, DAT, MKV, MP3, FLAC, M4V, FLV, Blu-ray, and more—no codecs or plugins required. Compact and efficient, it handles everything seamlessly.

Precise Playback Control

Adjust playback speed, subtitle and audio delays. Resume from where you left off with selected tracks, or start paused. Loop files indefinitely. Navigate frame by frame for exact control.

Video Enhancement

Fine-tune brightness, saturation, contrast, gamma, hue, noise reduction, and sharpness. Every detail optimized to your screen.

Audio Equalizer

Customize sound with precise EQ adjustments or presets like Large Hall, Party, Pop, Club, and Soft Rock. Tailored audio for any content or space.

Universal Format Support

Plays everything natively, including experimental H.266/VVC. Improved performance on demanding files. Fixed subtitle display on macOS Sequoia.

How It Works

1. Drag and Drop

Drag any video or audio file onto JustPlay. It launches instantly, no setup needed.

2. Play and Adjust

Hit play. Tweak speed, subtitles, or audio on the fly. Enhancements apply in real-time.

3. Fine-Tune Visuals and Sound

Adjust image settings and equalizer presets. Save preferences for future sessions.

4. Seamless Continuity

Resume playback exactly where you stopped, with all settings intact.
